Southern Served is a Southern-style restaurant located in the twin cities area. We specialize in cuisine from the East-Texas/Lousiana area. We offer spacious indoor seating as well as outdoor seating on our large patio. Our most popular menu item is the Cajun Crawfish by the pound. We welcome all guests to come get a taste of the South!
