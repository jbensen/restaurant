Restaurant Description

Southern Served is a Southern-style restaurant located in the twin cities area. We specialize in cuisine from the East-Texas/Lousiana area with some cajun influences. We have indoor and outdoor seating on our large patio. Our most popular menu item is the Cajun Crawfish by the pound. We welcome all guests. Come get a taste of the South!
